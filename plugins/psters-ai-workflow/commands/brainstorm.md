# Brainstorm

**Objective:** Explore a feature or problem, map risks and options, and produce a clear decision baseline for planning.

**Inputs:** Feature idea or problem to explore.

**Steps:**
1. Clarify what needs to be explored.
2. Gather relevant codebase context and known constraints.
3. Identify architecture options with trade-offs.
4. Resolve key open questions with the user.
5. Produce a concise decision baseline for `/plan`.

**Output:** Decision baseline document for `/plan`.
